## Replacing for production

For a real launch, replace every photo here with your own studio work:

1. **Hero / banner imagery** — your strongest signature frame.
2. **Selected work tiles (work-*.jpg)** — actual case studies.
3. **Studio rooms (studio-*.jpg)** — your own studio space.
4. **Team portraits (team-*.jpg)** — real faces of your team, always with permission.
5. **Journal images** — frames from your archive that match each note.

When replacing, keep the aspect ratios close to the originals (mostly 4:5 portrait and 4:3 landscape) so the layout stays tidy without CSS adjustment.
